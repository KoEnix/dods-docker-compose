Mission Brief
Axis troops have entrenched defenses setup throughout the town of Thala. If these defenses fall, the Allies will control another critical point in the push forward.

U.S. Army:
Pick up an explosives pack on your way into the city and plant it on one of the three objectives. Each objective destroyed will provide valuable extra time.
 
Wehrmacht:
Defend these targets at all costs! If Allied troops get through and plant explosives, you may have time to disarm them.
