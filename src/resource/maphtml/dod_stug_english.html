Mission Brief
It's early Sept. 1944 and the Allied offensive is in full swing after having freed the French city of Reims. The Germans, short on resources and military hardware, have set up a workshop to repair the few remaining Sturmgeschütz III (Stug) armored tank destroyers.

U.S. Army:
Eradicate the Germans from the town and capture the Stugs!
  
Wehrmacht:
Hold off the Allied attack and form a counterattack on Reims!
